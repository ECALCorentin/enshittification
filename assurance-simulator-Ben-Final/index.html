TITLE II: GUARANTEES AND SUBROGATION
Article 2.4: Procedures for Implementing "Non-Consecutive Consequential Loss" Coverage Coverage shall only apply after the exhaustion of the deductibles provided in the table of guarantees annexed hereto, and provided that the Insured provides formal proof of the direct and exclusive causal link between the originating event and the financial loss suffered. Article 3.2: Exclusions relating to the nature of the property The following are formally excluded from any coverage, without this list being considered exhaustive: Damage resulting from slow wear and tear, obsolescence, oxidation, corrosion, scale encrustation, or any slow-evolving climatic phenomenon not having the character of a natural event of abnormal intensity within the meaning of legislation on natural disasters.
